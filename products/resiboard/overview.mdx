ResiBoard is a <Tooltip tip="Property management software for landlords to track units, tenants, leases, and maintenance">property management</Tooltip> platform for landlords and residential managers. Manage units, <Tooltip tip="Lease agreements that define tenant occupancy, rent, and terms">leases</Tooltip>, maintenance requests, and payments from a single dashboard.

<Badge color="gray">Planned</Badge>
